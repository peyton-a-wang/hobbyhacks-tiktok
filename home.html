How to Play

TikTok Algor-ithms gamifies the experience of learning TikTok dances. Under "Learn," players are able to watch tutorials on TikTok dances that they would like to learn. Afterward, they can engage in the game under "Play," in which players can choose from dances, score points for hitting the right moves, and compete against their friends. Upon starting the game, players will need to use their computer camera and hit the targets at the appropriate times to score the maximum amount of points. More difficult moves will score more points, and players will be able to see where they stand in global rankings on various scoreboards.
