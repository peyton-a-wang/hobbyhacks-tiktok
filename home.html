How to Play

This gamified experience that we created is split into two sections. In "Learn," players are able to select TikTok dances which they would like to learn, and use their camera to record themselves dancing while watching a video of a perfected TikTok dancer. There are options to speed up or slow down the recordings of both yourself and the instructor in order to perfect certain moves. In "Play," players are able to choose dances to compete in and score points for hitting the right moves. Players will use their camera to record their dances, and our application will place targets on each player so they can see where they need to be in order to score the maximum amount of points. More difficult moves earn you more points! Players will be able to see where they stand in global rankings based on points with our scoreboard feature. Different scoreboards are available based on dance as well as an overall scoreboard. Do you think you can be the best TikToker?
